Code Counseling – Personal training for anything tech.

How

I am based in the Basel area. Meeting in person works best, but Skype is an option, too. I am a native German speaker and fluent in English. We can meet in your office, an open space, or a café, whichever works best for you.

In a first meeting, we get to know each other. We identify your goals and set the stage for working together towards the changes you are hoping for.

We then meet regularly, depending on your specific goals and schedule.

I charge roughly 200 CHF per hour. Sliding scale pricing for:
  • people paying out of their own pockets (as opposed to training being provided through an employer)
  • individuals from underrepresented groups
  • students (note that I work for FHNW, and therefore cannot teach FHNW students)
